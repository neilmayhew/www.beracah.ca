# Referrals

* [The MORE Network](https://more.outreach.ca/)
* [Lifespan Integration](https://www.lifespanintegration.com/)

PACCP and the MORE Network are both Christian bodies. You will need to contact MORE to get the names of counsellors as they're not listed on the website. 

Lifespan Integration is the therapeutic approach that I was trained in and I found that it was a very gentle yet very effective approach.
